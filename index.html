This is a general event planner that was made for the RHHS Prom Committee to keep track of enrollment in semi-formal events. It includes the profiling of attending students, their food preferences, and seating arrangements. This was made in collaboration with two other talented members

CHINESE CHECKERS CLIENT

In competition with 5 other groups, this program was made to battle with other AIs to win a collective game of Chinese checkers. I was in charge of making the AI portion, and it performed well! It won first in a grand total of 5/9 rounds (3/3 unofficial)

REMOTE MONITOR

If you've ever needed to monitor the activities of other computers on a LAN, you're in luck! This program records keystrokes of each connected computer in real-time, and can take screenshots, all multithreaded!
Disclaimer: I am not liable or responsible for unauthorized/inappropriate use of this utility. This program is meant to be a debugging tool, tech demonstration, and proof of concept

CLOCK SUITE

A 24-hour adjustable clock, housed in a cardboard box with binary LED indicators and a crystal LCD displaying the time. The clock also implements stopwatch and timer functionality, and can both at the same time concurrently alongside the global clock. The project is run off of an Arduino MEGA, and uses internal delays for synchronization

ARDUINO, C++

CHINESE CHECKERS CLIENT

In competition with 5 other groups, this program was made to battle with other AIs to win a collective game of Chinese checkers. I created our team's AI, winning first in a grand total of 5/9 rounds (3/3 unofficial). The algorithm implements a distance maximizing depth first search algorithm to determine optimal moves

RHHS EVENT PLANNER

A general event planner that was made for the RHHS Prom Committee to keep track of enrollment in semi-formal events. It includes the profiling of attending students, their food preferences, and seating arrangements.

This was made in collaboration as a team of three
